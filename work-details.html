SMART CONNECTIFY APP

Raven studio

In recent times, more focus has been given to innovative and enthusiastic approach towards solving the real time problems. To harness their talents, “Smart Odisha Hackathon-2022” involving participants across India was organized on November 30, 2022 and December 01, 2022 at Odisha University of Technology and Research (OUTR), Techno Campus, Kalinga Nagar, Ghatikia, Bhubaneswar on behalf of Skill Development and Technical Education Department, Government of Odisha.

About

This project addresses the issue of Non-Communicable Diseases (NCDs) in Odisha, where NCD-related deaths account for around 60% of total deaths. To tackle this, the government provides free medicines and testing, but public awareness is low, leading to out-of-pocket expenses for chronic patients.

The proposed solution is a real-time app that serves as a comprehensive resource for healthcare services in the state. It lists the quality of healthcare services and tags nearby facilities, enabling beneficiaries to easily access the information they need. By improving awareness and consistency in government health centers, the app has the potential to enhance healthcare quality and reduce expenses for NCD patients in Odisha.
